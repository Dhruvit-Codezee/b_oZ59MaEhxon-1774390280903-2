## Can Pipedrive be used for marketing?

Pipedrive is a top sales management tool designed to help teams manage intricate or lengthy sales processes. While it's primarily popular for allowing salespeople to focus on actions that close deals, it also greatly impacts marketing. Pipedrive gives marketers more control over the marketing workflow and makes collaboration with the sales team easier thanks to more direct information sharing.

## Functionality that can be used for marketing

Let's explore Pipedrive functions that marketers can leverage for better conversion rates and optimised marketing funnels.

### Email integration

According to the Content Marketing Institute, [87 percent](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/b2b-2021-research-final.pdf) of marketers use email marketing to disseminate their content. Email marketing with Pipedrive is easier to manage thanks to email automation, template creation, and storage. You can automatically send personalised emails to your prospects and leverage auto-reply functions for quick responses to customer inquiries.

### Deal labels

Keeping track of every interaction with leads can get overwhelming. With Pipedrive, you can get an overview of your activities for goal tracking and easily label each deal so you know where in the marketing funnel the prospect is. You will also be automatically notified when a deal stays stagnant for too long so you can follow up with the prospect.

### Lead interactions tracking

One of the most attractive features of Pipedrive is the user-friendly interface that allows you to create a custom workflow for your team. You can swiftly drag lead profiles from one part of the workflow to another, making it easy to track where in your sales funnel a prospect is.

After completing one stage, the lead's profile will automatically be moved to the next stage, or you can manually drag it. This functionality helps ensure no lead is overlooked or ignored.

### Task automation

Pipedrive marketing allows you to automate repetitive tasks that take up your marketing team's time, such as contact management, lead scoring, and even generating a deal when a new contact arises. You can create activities that Pipedrive will automatically initiate each time a deal is moved along your pipeline.

### Customised workflow

Pipedrive's visual pipeline prompts you to take action whenever there is movement along the marketing funnel. The workflow can be customised to share information on qualified leads with salespeople rather than a complex handover process.

### Time-savings

Most mundane and repetitive tasks take up most of a marketer's time. Pipedrive can help you automate these tasks, leaving more time for lead conversions.

### Easy integration into the sales process

Using Pipedrive for both marketing and sales activities makes collaboration easier. Pipedrive marketing automation makes for easy integration of the marketing funnel into the sales process.

## 7 Pipedrive integrations for marketing automation

Here are some of the top-ranked apps from Pipedrive Marketplace for marketing automation:

- **[Outfunnel](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/outfunnel/15695413b4ecd969)** - An integration platform connecting Pipedrive with marketing tools like MailChimp, ActiveCampaign, HubSpot, and web forms, making contact gathering and emailing simpler.
- **[ActiveDEMAND](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/active-demand/cb1e0c169f76c796)** - A marketing automation and call-tracking platform that integrates with Pipedrive to simplify tracking lead outreach efforts.
- **[Cliently](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/cliently/3df116d872e723e9)** - Integrates with Pipedrive to provide advanced email and marketing automation capabilities.
- **[Leady](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/leady/920c42067b7a4031)** - Works with Pipedrive to track interactions with B2B leads at every touchpoint, allowing easier lead qualifying and scoring.
- **[SmartSender.io](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/smart-sender-io/d56c6be7de1e1049)** - Helps gather insight from your customer journey and optimise experiences for email marketing.
- **[Zapier](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/zapier/10f6b602cda330ef)** - Enables seamless integration between Pipedrive and other apps through automatic information uploads and syncing lead databases.
- **[Intelli Messaging SMS](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/intelli-messaging-sms-australia/42a68717caf3a56d)** - Allows Pipedrive SMS integration, enabling automated text messages inside the Pipedrive platform.

## 3 best app combinations for complex automated marketing campaigns

### Automating an email campaign

Running an email campaign to boost revenue? Leverage Pipedrive integrations like **Zapier** to connect your contact management software, **Outfunnel** to automate emailing, and **SmartSender.io** to track your campaign outcomes.

### Automating an SMS campaign

Success lies in targeting the right prospects. Use **Leady** to measure lead quality, **Zapier** to connect a contact management system and filter out unverified numbers, and **Intelli Messaging SMS** to automate business text messaging.

### Automating a LinkedIn outreach campaign

Harness the power of LinkedIn with **Outreachly** to determine the right prospects, **Zapier** to enable contact database syncing with the CRM, and **Leady** to score leads.
