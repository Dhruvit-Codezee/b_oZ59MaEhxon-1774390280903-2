## 5 Types of Pipedrive Sales Reports

Different sales reports gather insights from multiple touchpoints in the sales funnel. Pipedrive enables you to generate the following types of sales management reports.

### Revenue by Salesperson Report

This report focuses on how much revenue each sales team member has generated. Such information is very useful as a performance indicator and enables you to help those lagging behind their individual goals.

This report usually contains:

- Quotas
- Sales goals
- Percent contributed to team revenue
- Number of deals closed
- Number of deals lost

### Conversion Rate Report

This essential report measures the percentage of deals won against the percentage of deals lost throughout a given period. It's useful in understanding if the effort put in by the sales team is generating results.

### Loss Rate by Sales Phase Report

As unfortunate as it is, businesses often lose deals near closure. This report highlights what stages deals are being lost at most. This helps you strategise and ensure more deals are closed at each phase of the sales journey.

### Average Sales Cycle Length Report

How long does it take your sales team to close a deal? This report answers this question, enabling you to determine the optimal time to do follow-ups with customers. It will also help you monitor performance, learning whether you are improving or need to adjust your approach to meet set sales goals.

### Lead Ageing Report

This report elaborates on the communication patterns between salespeople and leads. You can access detailed information like the date of the last contact and the outcomes of each interaction. You can utilise these essential observations to ensure prospects are getting value from salespeople at each stage of the sales funnel.

## 8 Apps and Tools to Create the Best Sales Reports in Pipedrive CRM

The [Pipedrive Marketplace](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace) provides businesses with ample trusted solutions compatible with CRM software. Some of the top sales reporting tools include:

### Sales Dashboards and Analytics by Dear Lucy

A sophisticated and user-friendly sales and revenue analytics solution for teams seeking to take control of their sales and revenue predictions. The app lets you create Pipedrive sales and revenue dashboards using its extensive library of sales KPIs. Add custom fields and filters to compare salesperson, product, or segment metrics.

### PandaDoc Proposals and eSignatures

This document automation app streamlines creating, approving, and eSigning proposals and contracts. It allows you to keep track of document statuses without leaving Pipedrive. Using document analytics, you can pick the best moment to follow up, displaying how your consumers reacted to each document.

### Quarte

This revenue forecasting app generates automatic projections in minutes using Pipedrive sales data. It allows you to develop a more accurate forecast and gain more control over your sales data.

### Skyvia

This integration platform connects Pipedrive to all popular cloud applications, databases, and flat files. You can use it to back up and restore your Pipedrive data.

### Ringostat

This service analyses phone calls to generate reports. It helps determine the efficacy of advertising channels and monitor the activity of a sales department.

### SyncHub

This app connects your reporting tools and dashboards to real-time Pipedrive data. Rather than CSV exports or copy/paste, you can merge your data in real-time to a secure data warehouse. Simply connect your preferred reporting tool (such as PowerBI, Tableau, or even Microsoft Excel) and create the dashboards and insights you need to help your organisation develop.

### Graphy

This data visualisation platform helps create clear data presentations accessible to the team. It can be linked to your team's use of Pipedrive and other data sources like Google Analytics, Stripe, and PostgreSQL. It's quick, elegant, and collaborative.

### Pointagram

This app gamifies your team's goals for tracking and increasing motivation. You can introduce entertaining competitions based on your unique aims to increase team engagement.
