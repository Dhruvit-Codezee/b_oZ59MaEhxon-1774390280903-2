## What Are AI Agents?

AI agents are **autonomous software programmes** that can perceive their environment, make decisions, and act to achieve specific objectives.

### Key Features

- **Autonomy:** Work independently with minimal human input
- **Reusability:** Adapt to multiple business tasks
- **Task Decomposition:** Break down complex processes into manageable parts
- **Learning Capability:** Improve continuously with new data and feedback

### Types of AI Agents

- **Reactive and rule-based agents:** Operate on predefined logic
- **Deliberative and learning-based agents:** Plan, reason, and evolve over time

Unlike traditional automation tools, AI agents provide **context-aware, scalable automation**, making them more powerful than standard chatbots or RPA.
