## What is CRM? Why Does a Business Need a CRM?

Customer relationship management (CRM) is software that helps businesses manage their interactions with customers. These programs enable businesses to see the big picture of their customer base, streamline communications and efficiently analyse data to take action on opportunities.

Adopting CRM can help your business grow by tracking what marketing strategies work best for reaching new customers and understanding your customers better and how they use your services or products.

## Pipedrive CRM Key Features

Like other sales CRM, Pipedrive is used to manage customer relationships. Keeping track of customers, their preferences, and everything related to them can be a monumental task for any business. And when you throw in another competitor, multiple locations, and various marketing campaigns into the mix, things can get even more challenging.

That's where sales software like Pipedrive comes in - to make things much simpler. Here are the four Pipedrive features that help with that:

### Key Feature 1: Workflow Automation

[Pipedrive's workflow automation](/blog/pipedrive-sales-automation-guide) is one of its pillar features. Sales processes are more complex than ever before. As a result, sales reps struggle to keep track of leads and follow up consistently. They spend way too much time on unimportant manual tasks and not enough time on high-value activities that drive revenue.

You can automate every aspect of your sales journey with Pipedrive's workflow automation features, from:

- Scheduling meetings
- Moving deals to the next stage automatically
- Creating new activities, e.g., creating a "Call" activity when a new deal is created
- Notifying the sales manager when the deal is marked won/lost
- Automatically following up with lost deals via email
- Creating new deals when existing ones enter specific stages of the pipeline with Zapier integration

Workflow automation is based on **triggers** and **events**. For instance, if a trigger event occurs (e.g. when a new deal is created), the system sends an automated email through the synced email account.

### Key Feature 2: Video Conferencing

Pipedrive CRM integrates with tons of [video conferencing apps](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/apps/video-calls-solutions). It is invaluable to meet with potential customers or suppliers in real-time, hold webinars, and show them how your products or services work. It cuts out travel time and expenses by enabling face-to-face meetings from the comfort of your office.

With Pipedrive CRM, you can integrate with the following video conferencing apps:

- Microsoft Teams
- Zoom
- Google Meet
- Bonjoro
- Ciara
- Verticalls

### Key Feature 3: Customisable Reports

Pipedrive CRM offers visual reports in detailed charts and tables for a detailed overview of your team's performance. Using Pipedrive as your team reporting software streamlines the process of collating data from multiple sources into one central location where all team members can access it anytime and share updates when necessary.

### Key Feature 4: Customised Email Templates

Creating new emails all the time feels a little like trying to hit a moving target. What works for some people will not necessarily work for your customers; what worked last month might not be as effective next month.

Pipedrive's customised email templates eliminate the need to create new templates each time a new lead pops into your sales process. You can find the email templates on the Email tab in your Pipedrive account.

## Five Pipedrive CRM Benefits for Business Growth

Today's digitally savvy customers expect more from your business. They want to collaborate with you, get to know you better as a person, and even become friends with you. That's why to stay ahead of the competition, your company must connect with your customers on a personal level. Here are some Pipedrive CRM benefits your business should take advantage of:

### 1. Efficient Customer Behaviour Tracking

Pipedrive enables users to keep detailed information about:

- Customer conversations
- What they've purchased in the past
- How they prefer to engage with your brand
- How they interact with your customer service team

Efficient tracking of customer behaviour enables you to provide better services to them. You will be able to respond to complaints, solve issues more quickly, and provide a better customer experience overall.

### 2. Consolidate Your Marketing Efforts

Having all of your marketing efforts laid out on one dashboard will help you see how different approaches impact each other and affect your bottom line. Pipedrive displays a detailed overview of your sales pipeline on one dashboard. Every sales team member with access to the company account can see what's going on and what steps to take to close the pending deals.

### 3. Improve Sales and Marketing Collaboration

Pipedrive improves your sales and marketing collaboration by having all your sales and marketing information in one place. The CRM includes tools for collaboration, such as video-conferencing tools where teams of all sizes can host important meetings in real-time and share relevant information to make better business decisions.

### 4. Automate Tedious and Time-Consuming Tasks

Automation can help save time and boost efficiency in many areas of your business. For example, using an automated email builder enables you to create emails more quickly and send them almost instantly.

With Pipedrive, you won't have to wait for a designer to create the email template and a copywriter to write it whenever you want to welcome a new customer.

### 5. Understand Your Most Profitable Customers

Understanding the customers who are most profitable for your business may seem like a difficult task, but it's easier than you might think. With a sales CRM for teams, you can see which customers have purchased from you and which have been the most profitable.

This data can help you invest more time and energy into serving these customers and nurturing new customers who have never bought from you. After all, selling to an existing customer has a probability between [60% and 70%](https://www.semrush.com/blog/customer-retention-stats/).
