## Best Sales Pipedrive Integrations From Pipedrive Marketplace

Pipedrive is a leading developer and creator of enterprise-grade software to drive digital transformation and improve business performance. Below are some of the best integrations from the [Pipedrive Marketplace](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace) for [sales automation](/blog/pipedrive-sales-automation-guide).

> "SDRs now have to make more touches per lead and make those touches more relevant and personalised. It takes 18 dials to connect with a buyer. Only 23.9% of sales emails are opened." - Gartner Report: Sales Development Technology

### 1. Leadfeeder

[Leadfeeder](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/leadfeeder/82d974d85138484b) is a sales intelligence tool that shows which companies visit your website. It helps build a targeted list of leads you can feed to Pipedrive.

You can view the visitor data from each company in Leadfeeder, including their location, the pages they viewed, and how many times they visited your site. It lets you check whether a lead is genuinely interested in your product or service before exporting it to Pipedrive.

### 2. Seamless.AI

[Seamless.AI](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/seamless-ai/7301d9e368468ad2) is an AI-powered business intelligence application that helps you find new prospects and grow your company faster. It provides automated email addresses, direct phone numbers, real-time decision-maker updates, and more directly into your Pipedrive.

Stop wasting time reaching out to the wrong person or having a conversation that doesn't align with your interests. Instead, start every conversation with a contact who is already interested in what you offer and ready to take the next step.

### 3. Aeroleads

[Aeroleads](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/aero-leads/9c26cb7a137de504) is an automated lead generation Pipedrive integration app that enables you to find the business email addresses of your prospects. It obtains emails and phone numbers from LinkedIn, Crunchbase, Angellist, Twitter, and other sites.

You can find the necessary business email addresses in just a few clicks. You can even enrich the contact information with details like name, company name, job title, phone number, and more.

### 4. Prospect.io (Overloop)

Prospecting can be a pain, but Prospect.io's (now called [Overloop](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/overloop/0e004b418ee1fc36)) Chrome extension makes it easy to find new contacts while you're browsing the web. Just activate the extension and tell it what you're looking for - it'll automatically find potential leads. You can then send those leads right from Prospect.io or export them to Pipedrive.

You can also use Prospect.io to set up drip campaigns and follow-up sequences. It gives you an alternative to Pipedrive's built-in follow-up feature.

### 5. Drip

[Drip](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/drip/a3fe21c3fc7d9f01) is a marketing automation platform that lets you deliver the right message to your leads at the right time. It provides tools to identify, nurture, and convert leads into customers. You can also use Drip to send personalised product recommendations to customers based on their engagement with your business.

Pipedrive integrations with Drip help you work more efficiently and close more deals.

### 6. Outfunnel

Connect Pipedrive with [Outfunnel](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/outfunnel/15695413b4ecd969) to get the most out of your email marketing. You can use Outfunnel's real-time reporting to view your email campaign performance alongside your Pipedrive sales activity. Its two-way integration makes importing new leads from lead capture forms easy and sending your best prospects targeted email sequences from Pipedrive.

### 7. Klenty

[Klenty](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/klenty/4ea6d81e4599a462) is a Pipedrive sales automation tool that saves time by automating manual tasks like sending personalised emails and follow-ups to leads. It enables your sales team to focus on closing more deals with less effort. You can also use Klenty to automatically stay in touch with your leads at scale.

Klenty integrates with Pipedrive to automatically create new contacts or push them into the right stage based on their engagement.

### 8. Zapier

[Zapier](https://www.pipedrive.com/en/marketplace/app/zapier/10f6b602cda330ef) connects Pipedrive with hundreds of your favourite apps so you can automatically send information back and forth between them, saving time and effort. For example, if you want to set triggers to send an email to your sales reps every time a new deal is opened in the Pipedrive sales funnel, Zapier can do this automatically.
