## How to Get Started in Pipedrive

Before we dive any deeper, let's start with the most common questions for beginners:

- **What is Pipedrive?** Pipedrive is a sales automation CRM that has served the sales department for over a decade.
- **What does Pipedrive do?** Pipedrive helps businesses grow and reach their sales goals faster. It's used by companies of all sizes as the primary CRM tool, as well as an add-on to other sales and marketing automation solutions.
- **How does Pipedrive work?** Pipedrive is a web-based tool that provides a neat dashboard where sales experts can track their deals.

As an expanding company, you need a sales CRM that grows with you. Pipedrive provides access to all the tools necessary to build your business empire.

### Set Up Your Pipedrive Account

New users can register for a Pipedrive account from a smartphone or computer. The platform offers a 14-day free trial to test the full features with no credit card commitment. All you have to provide is your work email to open an account and set up your password. If you were a Pipedrive user with an account, you could easily toggle between the different accounts by clicking the profile icon and selecting **Switch account**.

Companies can also add new users to the Pipedrive account by going to **Settings > Manage Users > Users**. Locate the "Add Users" button on the top right side of the screen and enter the emails of the person (or people, separated by a comma) you want to add. Click **Preview** and check the total cost based on the number of users on your account.

## How to Organise and Set Up Transactions, Contacts, and Actions

Every software has a bit of a learning curve, and Pipedrive workflow automation is no different. New users may take a while to get used to the controls, load new contacts, add new pipelines, activities, and track deals.

### Import and Sync Data from Multiple Sources

Pipedrive allows users to import and sync data from multiple sources, including:

- XLSX, CSV, and XLS spreadsheet files
- From a previous CRM with Import2

#### Spreadsheet Files (XLSX, CSV, XLS)

Microsoft Excel is one of the oldest yet widely used business management tools. To load data from a spreadsheet to your Pipedrive account:

1. Fill in the mandatory fields: Leads, Deals, People, Organisation, Products, Notes, and Activities. Each mandatory field should have its column listed on the spreadsheet.
2. Create custom fields for any extra information you want to include about your deal.
3. Import your file by going to **Settings > Import data > From File**.
4. Map the spreadsheet columns onto the Pipedrive fields. Pipedrive automatically does this for you but may require manual assistance when mapping.

#### From a Previous CRM with Import2

If you happen to be a user of another sales CRM with historical data from past deals, you can move all that data to your Pipedrive dashboard in minutes with [Import2](https://www.import2.com/):

1. Connect your Pipedrive account to Import2 and get a quote by moving a sample.
2. Set up the users and pipelines in your Pipedrive account to keep the migration process as simple as possible.
3. Enter your logins to the previous CRM on Import2 and click **"Test Connection"**.
4. Start the import and review the progress directly from Import2.

You can also import data from your Mailchimp account by exporting it as a CSV file and then uploading it as a spreadsheet.

### Organise Your Lead Information on the Deals Page

One of the greatest advantages of Pipedrive workflow automation is how it summarises all the information you have on your leads in one dashboard. Generally, you can organise prospect information into the following categories:

- **Leads** - Pipedrive allows you to organise lead information in a "Lead box". Here, prequalified leads are siphoned from the mass. Pipedrive's Lead Inbox allows you to store lead data in order of priority, from the most urgent to the least urgent.
- **People and Organisational Contacts** - Pipedrive recognises people as the actual sales individuals and organisations generally as where your leads work. You can gather names, email addresses, phone numbers, email conversations, detailed notes, and scheduled activities.
- **Deals** - Deals are the ultimate reward for your hard efforts. Pipedrive can help you track every deal, every transaction, and every step your team takes until that deal is closed or lost. You can estimate the winning or losing probability and assign each deal to an "owner".
- **Activities** - An activity is any action you take to move a lead through the sales funnel. Pipedrive's default activities include lunch, meeting, email, call, task, and deadline. You can also set customised activities at **Settings > Company settings > Activity Types**.

## How to Configure Personal and Corporate Settings

Pipedrive's features can be used by individuals and enterprises with tens of users. Configuring your personal and corporate settings means defining who should have access to user management, personal settings, security settings, and other sensitive information.

### Configuring Personal Settings

To configure personal settings, open your account and go to **Settings > Manage Users > Users** to see who has access to sensitive information on your account.

#### Syncing Your Email and Calendar

Pipedrive's email sync feature will track real-time communications with your leads. The platform takes it further by allowing its built-in email feature to filter out the marketing emails that need the most attention. The calendar sync feature lets you monitor your scheduled activities directly from your Pipedrive account.

### Configuring Corporate Settings

Company settings can only be altered if you have account settings access. These settings grant access to billing settings, company information edit access, and more. Company settings on Enterprise and Professional plans also offer features such as adding users to teams, changing permissions, and setting access rights.

## Pipedrive Billing Process

Log into your Pipedrive account and go to **Billing > Add billing details**. To change anything before setting up your billing account, go to **Manage Users** and edit the information. Pipedrive lets you modify your plan and billing cycle from the Manage Subscription button. Enter your billing information, choose your payment options, review your purchase, and you're set.

## How Pipedrive Guarantees the Privacy and Security of Your Data

Pipedrive includes security features that safeguard your data and protect your company's reputation. Key security points:

- You are responsible as the data controller when using Pipedrive to process client data.
- Pipedrive is **GDPR compliant** when handling personal data about EU residents or within the EU.
- Pipedrive ensures the safe transfer of data within and outside the EEA through third-party EU-US Privacy Shield frameworks and EU Commission clauses.
- Pipedrive established a **data centre in Europe** to assure users of compliance with GDPR.
