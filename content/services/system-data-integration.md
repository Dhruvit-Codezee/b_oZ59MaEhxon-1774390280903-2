## Overview

Disconnected systems limit the effectiveness of AI and automation.

Automaly integrates CRMs, internal tools, and data sources to reduce manual work, improve data accuracy, and create a reliable foundation for automation. Integration work is scoped through the AI Readiness Assessment to ensure it directly supports priority business outcomes.

## What We Integrate

We work with all major platforms and can connect virtually any system:

- CRM platforms (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Airtable)
- Marketing tools (Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Google Ads)
- Operational tools (Slack, Monday.com, Notion, Asana)
- Financial systems (Xero, QuickBooks, Stripe)
- Custom APIs and internal databases

## The Result

A unified data ecosystem where information flows seamlessly between systems, eliminating manual data entry, reducing errors, and providing a single source of truth for decision-making.
