## Overview

As organisations grow, operational complexity increases. Manual handovers, duplicated work, and inconsistent processes quickly become barriers to scale.

We design and automate operational workflows to improve reliability, visibility, and efficiency across teams. Automation is applied only after processes are refined, ensuring long-term performance rather than short-term fixes.

## Our Approach

Automaly takes a process-first approach to operational automation:

1. Map existing workflows to identify bottlenecks and redundancies
2. Refine and standardise processes before applying automation
3. Implement automation with appropriate monitoring and alerts
4. Measure impact and iterate for continuous improvement

## Common Applications

- Employee onboarding and offboarding workflows
- Project handover and task routing automation
- Approval chains and sign-off processes
- Inventory and resource management
- Internal communication and notification systems
