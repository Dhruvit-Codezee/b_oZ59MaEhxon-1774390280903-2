## Overview

Custom AI Agents are designed to handle structured, repeatable tasks across your organisation.

Automaly builds AI agents that integrate into existing workflows to support decision-making, automate internal processes, and reduce operational load. Each agent is designed with appropriate controls, transparency, and governance to ensure safe and reliable use at scale.

## How We Build AI Agents

Every AI agent we develop follows a structured approach:

- Deep understanding of the workflow it will support
- Integration with your existing tools and data sources
- Built-in governance, audit trails, and transparency
- Iterative testing and refinement before deployment
- Ongoing monitoring and optimisation

## Use Cases

AI agents can be deployed across a range of business functions:

- Content generation and scheduling workflows
- Customer enquiry triage and routing
- Data extraction and processing pipelines
- Internal knowledge retrieval and Q&A
- Automated reporting and insight generation
